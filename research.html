Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and human cognition.

Multi-Modal Learning

Inspired by how humans naturally process information from different modalities, I develop systems that can understand and reason across diverse inputs like vision, audio, and text.

Continual Learning

I'm fascinated by how humans can continuously acquire new knowledge without forgetting previous information. My work explores how AI systems can do the same.

Large Language Models

I investigate how LLMs can be enhanced for more faithful, interpretable, and personalized interactions while maintaining safety.

Current Projects

LLM Persuasion Arena - A platform for testing persuasion techniques with different LLM agents, exploring personalization and evaluation methods.

Multi-Modal Earthquake Prediction - Integrating diverse seismic data sources into unified AI-driven systems for improved prediction and early warning.

Emotion Recognition - Cross-modal fusion models combining audio and video features for robust emotion understanding.
